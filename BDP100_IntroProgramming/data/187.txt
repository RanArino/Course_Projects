Gary W. Giesbrecht, President and CEO of MacDon Industries Ltd., is pleased to announce the appointment of Gene G. Fraser to the position of Vice President effective January 1, 2014.In his new role, Mr. Fraser will be responsible for MacDon’s Global Sales and Marketing functions.Mr. Fraser joined MacDon in June of 1981 and has held several positions throughout the Company and most recently Director of Marketing.Mr. Fraser’s farm background and many years of experience in agribusiness is an asset in his new role. He is a graduate of St. Francis Xavier University and holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration.MacDon Industries Ltd. is a family owned, Canadian based corporation, specializing in the design and manufacture of harvesting equipment for global markets.www.macdon.com
