Pop superstar Lady Gaga has postponed a concert Friday night at Rogers Arena in Vancouver.A message on her website says the singer is suffering from severe bronchitis.The ailment also forced Lady Gaga to postpone her scheduled performance in Seattle Wednesday evening.The website asks fans to keep their tickets since they will be valid for rescheduled performances in each city.The website did not say when those shows will be held.Lady Gaga is scheduled to perform in Toronto, Ottawa, Montreal and Quebec City in early July.
